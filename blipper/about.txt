[b]Demo Instructions[/b]                                


1.  Rotate the control knob to adjust the 
colored bars.

2.  Pressing the control knob advances to the next
color:  [color=ff0000]RED[/color], [color=00ff00]GREEN[/color], [color=ffff00]YELLOW[/color].

3.  The [color=5555ff]BLUE[/color] bar indicates Received Signal Strength.

4.  After a few seconds of inactivity, the control
knob will go to sleep.  Rotate again to wake it.

5.  If the unit malfunctions, try the Reboot or
Shutdown buttons on the display.
